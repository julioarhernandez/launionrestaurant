WHO WE ARE & HISTORY

Marta, the owner of La Union Restaurant, is a cook passioned who was born in Salvador. She has had an interest in culinary arts since she was very young. When Marta decided to chase the American Dream and flew to Miami, she thought to open a Salvadoran Restaurant in this City and brings to American people the culinatry culture of El Salvador.

After several years of effort, she reaches her dream and opened La Union Restaurant with her husband Fulanito.
